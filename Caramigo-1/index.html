about

Caramigo are Edel Perth and Noni Espinosa. They met while studying music over 20 years ago. Through the years they have collaborated writing their own songs with a smooth feel and funky style, they have over 100 songs to date.

Always well recieved, their original songs tell tales of life, love and longing in Edel's Irish background is complemented by Noni's Chilian roots, the music is interwoven with a funky South American groove.

Caramigo have performed at many festivals and venues throughout South Australia making their debut performance at The Grace Emily in 2000.

Using both the Irish and Spanish words for friend (cara & amigo) the name Caramigo was born.

Caramigo have released 2 albums to date 'As is' in 2005 & 'Unravel' in 2016.
